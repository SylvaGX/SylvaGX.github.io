Hanabi

Hanabi is a cooperative card game in which you have to cooperate with the other people in the game. This game is different from other games because games are typically competitive. But in this game, when you lose, everyone loses. So you have to cooperate to all of you win the game. You can play this game with 2 or 4 players.

Sopa de Letras

Sopa de Letras is a game in which you have a soup of letters. In this soup, you have words in some directions. Depending on the algorithm, you have left-to-right, top-to-bottom, right-to-left, bottom-to-top, and diagonals. The objective is to find all the words in the soup.
